About Insta Claim

At Insta Claim, we are dedicated to transforming healthcare experiences for patients, providers, and insurance companies. Our mission is to simplify and enhance patient care with cutting-edge solutions that focus on accessibility, efficiency, and compassion.

Seamless ABHA Integration

Our platform connects effortlessly with the Ayushman Bharat Health Account (ABHA), giving users secure access to their medical records and enabling digital insurance claims submission.

Instant Insurance Verification

We streamline verification processes, ensuring healthcare providers and patients receive immediate and accurate insurance coverage details, minimizing administrative burdens.

Secure Data Sharing

Our platform enables secure and controlled medical data exchange, enhancing care coordination and eliminating unnecessary paperwork.

Empowering Patients

We equip individuals with valuable resources and personalized support, helping them make informed health decisions with confidence.

At Insta Claim, we believe in a patient-first approach, creating a future where healthcare is seamless, efficient, and centered around individual needs.
